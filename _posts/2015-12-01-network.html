2014

In 2014 we started the program with 13 German cities: Berlin, Hamburg, Munich, Cologne, Münster, Paderborn, Leipzig, Dresden,Chemnitz, Gießen, Heilbronn, Stuttgart and Ulm.

 2015

7 more OK Labs were launched in 2015, they are: Bonn, Magdeburg, Jena, Frankfurt, Wuppertal, Karlsruhe and Freiburg.

 2016

This year we welcome the cities and regions Kassel, Erlangen, Hannover, Düsseldorf, Ruhr district and Lower Rhine. That makes a total of 25 OK Labs in Germany!
